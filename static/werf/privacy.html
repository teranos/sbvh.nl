Werf — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 April 2026

This policy describes how SBVH ("we") handles personal data in connection with Werf, a job matching service.

Controller

SBVH
KvK: 86374931
Contact: privacy@sbvh.nl

What we collect

  • CV documents you upload (PDF)
  • Structured text extracted from your CV
  • Motivation letters you create within the service
  • Basic usage data (timestamps, feature interactions)

How we process it

  • PDF parsing happens on your device. The raw file is not sent to our servers.
  • Structured text extracted from your CV is sent to our servers for analysis.
  • Analysis is performed using third-party AI providers. Your data is sent to them for processing.
  • No recruiter sees your data without your explicit consent.

Legal basis

We process your data based on your consent (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a)). You can withdraw consent at any time.

Third parties

We use third-party AI providers to analyse structured text from your CV. These providers process data on our behalf under data processing agreements.

Retention

Your data is retained for as long as you use the service. Upon account deletion or withdrawal of consent, your data is deleted within 30 days.

Your rights

Under GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent
  • Lodge a complaint with the Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens

Contact

For privacy-related questions: privacy@sbvh.nl
