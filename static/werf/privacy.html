Werf — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 April 2026

This policy describes how SBVH ("we") handles personal data in connection with Werf, a job matching service.

Controller

SBVH
KvK: 86374931
Contact: privacy@sbvh.nl

What we collect

  • Identity: Name and email address obtained through LinkedIn sign-in via AWS Cognito
  • CV data: PDF documents and structured text extracted from them
  • Device tokens: Push notification tokens, used solely to send you updates about your application status
  • Usage metrics: Timestamps and feature interactions

Data sharing

Your data is processed by the following categories of third parties, each under data processing agreements:

  • Identity provider: AWS Cognito (authentication) and LinkedIn (sign-in)
  • AI analysis provider: For structured CV parsing

Recruiters cannot access your information without your explicit permission.

Legal basis

We process your data based on your consent (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a)). You can withdraw consent at any time.

Retention

Your data is retained for as long as you use the service. Upon account deletion or withdrawal of consent, your data is deleted within 30 days.

Your rights

Under GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent
  • Lodge a complaint with the Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens

Contact

For privacy-related questions: privacy@sbvh.nl
